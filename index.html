We decided to fix this.
Each case is different. Each customer is different.
The goal of this role is not simply to offer fancy solution and methods in a nice looking format. Well yes, that’s done also. But rather me and my fellows make sure we deliver what we say we deliver with methods we say we use. There’s no transition between sales and delivery.
